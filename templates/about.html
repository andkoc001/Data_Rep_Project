Industry 4.0

The term Industry 4.0 is a shorthand for the forth industry revolution. It means the unification of the real world of production machines with the virtual world of the Internet and information technology. It involves the information exchange between the machines, and IT systems (Internet of Things). Its application extends also beyond production andmay encompas, among others, continuous machinery diagonstics and promote overall equipment efficiency.

Autonomous Equipment Maintenance

Equipment degrades all the time and need to be maintained continuously in order to fulfill its mission (Operating Determinants of Maintaining the Operational State of Container Terminal Equipment).

Application fo the Industry 4.0, Internet of Things, Big Data and related technologies for equipment maintenance allows for the process automation (Autonomous Maintenance: What It Is and Why It Matters - ReliablePlant). The stream of data coming from the condition monitoring sensors after suitable processing enhance the chance to recognise the optimal time for pre-brakedown intervention, and so improving the equipment lifecycle. The data when coupled with suitable machine learning algorithms, may bring numerous benefits (AI and Machine Learning engines for industry), such as failure prediction (predicts probability of future failures with historical data plus current conditions) or anomaly detection (analyzes current conditions to identify deviations from normal operating behavior).
